Robert Downey Jr.

The Iron Man

Downey at a Comic Convention promoting the release of Avengers 2: Age of Ultron, circa 2014.

Robert Downey Jr. is an actor appearing in over 60 movies in his long, successful career. Born in Manhattan, New York City on April 4, 1965, Robert Downey Jr. has played many beloved characters such as Sherlock Holmes, Charlie Chaplan, and, most recently, Tony Stark aka Iron Man. Robert is massively successful and beloved by both fans and his fellow colleagues and it is hard to believe that there was a time where he had battled his drug addiction and depression.

During the late 90s and early 2000s, his drug addiction had reached a level which had interfered with both his professional and personal life. He had landed himself in jail multiple times, many film productions considered him a liability, and he was losing everything. Robert Downey Jr. was heading below zero. In 2001, he, finally, decided to regain control of his life and went to rehab and made a comeback launching him to super-stardom.

Robert Downey Jr. has had a long and successful career, made a super hero comeback, and is an all around amazing man. His story is inspiring and show us all that just because we hit rock bottom it does not mean we have to stay there.
